Tasks now roll into Today automatically when their due date arrives. Incomplete tasks whose deadline is today or earlier move into the Today list on load and at midnight (including when the machine wakes past midnight), instead of waiting for a manual transfer. A new "Auto-roll due tasks" toggle in goal settings turns it off; manual transfer counts are unaffected.
