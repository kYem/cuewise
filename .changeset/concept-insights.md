Add a Concepts tab to the Insights page that shows your spaced-repetition progress at a glance: how many cards are due now, your deck's composition (new / learning / mastered), a retention measure, average ease, a 7-day due forecast, and which cards need attention. A small due-count badge now also appears on the concepts nav. Everything is derived from your existing review schedule — nothing new is stored.
